Rules
1. All ducks race together. Finishing order determines points.
2. No touching the water or ducks once released.
3. If a duck gets stuck, assistance is allowed only by throwing an object.
4. Disputes are settled by group vote.
5. Commissioner (Mandy) has final say if needed.
